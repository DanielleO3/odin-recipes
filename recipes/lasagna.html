Zoodle Lasagne

Description

I'm a pasta lover to the core, but never consumed veggie filled pasta. I wanted to try something different and came across zucchini noodles, also known as "Zoodles". Zoodles are a delicious healthy gluten-free pasta alternative made from zucchini! The best part of zoodles, they are good for you and actually taste really good.

This Zuchinni Noodle Lasagna is easy to make. Made with fresh zucchini noodles instead of regular pasta

Ingredients
  • 4 zucchini
  • 1 ½ cups homemade or store-bought tomato sauce
  • ⅔ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 ½ cups bechamel sauce
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped
